Almost all the content for this lessons is in the slides (link in the [README.md](../README.md)) 

There is one hands-on exercise that guides learners through how to calculate a return on investment (ROI) for a wind farm predictive maintenance project (case study used throughout the lesson). All information for this exercise is in the notebook. 

Main point to emphasize during notebook exercise: 
- The exercise is not about the math, but about the process of calculating the ROI (so ask for help if needed)
- The exercise will ask them to calculate ROI for the first year of the project. And as a stretch goal, to calculate for 3 years (which results in much higher ROI), emphasize the importance of projecting ROI over the expected lifetime of the project.
